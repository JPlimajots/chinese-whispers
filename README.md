# ☎️ Chinese Whispers
> *"Connect the dots, decode the chaos, and try not to send the mafia to the bakery."*

---

## 📜 PATENT RECORD: TELEPHONE EXCHANGE No. 1926
Welcome to the manual switching center of **Chinese Whispers**, a game developed for the **[GameDev.tv Game Jam 2026](https://itch.io/jam/gamedevtv-jam-2026)**. 

The year is 1920. Technology roars through copper wires and solid wood panels. You've been hired as the city's chief operator. Your objective? Route calls with precision, manage your patience, and survive your probationary shift.

---

## 🔌 CROSSED WIRES: THE THEME
This year's Game Jam theme is **Connections**. In *Chinese Whispers*, we explore this concept in three vital layers:

1. **The Physical Connection (Tactile):** Interact with the classic mechanics of an antique switchboard. Physically drag electrical cables, stretch them across the board under pressure, and manage mechanical relays.
2. **The Narrative Connection (The Butterfly Effect):** Communication is fragile. Ambiguities and period slang fill the air. A wrong connection results in systemic chaos, creating absurd dialogues and unintended consequences.
3. **The Auditory Connection (Immersion):** Featuring a full-body sound design—from the heavy clack of relay switches and the satisfying plug-in of cables to the frantic ringing of bells and the creeping static of misconnections.

## 🕹️ OPERATING MANUAL (HOW TO PLAY)

| Command | Mechanical Action | Technical Feedback |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Left Click (Drag)** | `Connect / Select` | Plugs the transmission line; confirms call attendance (on the phone). |
| **Right Click** | `Cancel / Unplug` | Retracts cables and cancels connections; silences static feedback. |
| **Spacebar** | `Confirm` | Used to answer calls and advance the dialogue narrative. |
| **ESC Key** | `Quit` | Abandons your post and exits the exchange. |

---

## 💼 CAREER PROGRESSION
Your performance determines your future. Each successful connection advances your narrative progress, while failure to manage call patience or routing to the wrong destination will result in warnings from the HR department. Stay sharp: three strikes and you’ll be summarily fired.
