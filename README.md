# ☎️ Chinese Whispers
> *"Connect the dots, decode the chaos, and try not to send the mafia to the bakery."*

---

## 📜 PATENT RECORD: TELEPHONE EXCHANGE No. 1926
Welcome to the manual switching center of **Chinese Whispers**, a game developed especially for the **[GameDev.tv Game Jam 2026](https://itch.io/jam/gamedevtv-jam-2026)**. 

The year is 1920. Technology roars through copper wires and solid wood panels. You've been hired as the city's chief operator, and your sanity (as well as your wallet) is on the line.

---

## 🔌 CROSSED WIRES: THE THEME
This year's Game Jam theme is **Connections**. In *Chinese Whispers*, we explore this concept in two vital layers:

1. **The Physical Connection (Tactile):** The player directly interacts with the classic mechanics of an antique switchboard. Each call requires you to physically grab an electrical cable, stretch it across the board under severe time pressure, and plug it into the correct jack.
2. **The Narrative Connection (The Butterfly Effect):** Communication is a fragile web. The citizens' requests are full of ambiguities and period slang. A wrong connection doesn't just result in a silent "Game Over"; it connects the wrong lifelines, generating systemic chaos and absurd dialogues that shape the course of the day (and incur heavy fines on your paycheck!).

---

## 🕹️ OPERATING MANUAL (HOW TO PLAY)

| Command | Mechanical Action | Technical Documentation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Left Click (Drag)** | `Connect Cable` | Selects the caller's origin pin and stretches the transmission line to the destination jack. |
| **Right Click** | `Unplug Line` | Instantly cancels the current dragging action, retracting the cable to avoid misconnections. |
| **ESC Key** | `Abandon Post (Quit)` | Closes the operating panel and immediately ends the shift. |
